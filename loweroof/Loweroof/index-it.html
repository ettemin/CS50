What
We
Offer

1.

Deal sourcing

Thanks to our Proprietary Software, we can scout thousands of investment opportunities daily.

2.

Valuation

Through the acquisition of relevant information (e.g cadastral documents, appraisals, debt position...) we evaluate each property from an economic, architectural, and legal point of view.

3.

Property inspection

We arrange a property view with the tribunal to assess the property's condition, including its heating and cooling systems, plumbing, electrical work, water, and sewage, as well as some fire and safety issues.

4.

Financials

After the Due Diligence process, we evaluate the best financing option for the deal and identify all the hidden and ancillary costs associated with the auction process.

5.

Execution

We attend the auction for our investors and manage the process until the keys' handover (including vacating the property if occupied).

6.

Renovations

Usually, we only advise properties with minimal refurbishment needs (e.g. painting, selected furniture and lightning replacement...). Nonetheless, in the case of "value-add" investments, we manage the whole renovation project.

7.

Revenue realization

Whether you want to sell the property or rent it out, we help you market it and maximize your profit by finding the best buyer or tenant.
