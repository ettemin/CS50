Why Italy?

Lower prices

On average, Italian real estate market is the cheapest among the major European countries

*Average Transaction Price of a New Dwelling.
Source: Deloitte Property Index 2022

Higher Growth

While still low compared to other European major cities, Milan’s real estate prices rose +40% in the last 5 years

*Cumulative Average Reale Estate Price Growth per Country since 2015
*Source: OECD Economic Outlook

Tax benefits

High-net-worth individuals moving their tax residence to Italy are enabled to apply a substitute tax to their foreign income, amounting to €100,000 for each fiscal year, in lieu of the Italian Income Tax

*Italian Consolidated Law on Income Tax (TUIR), art. 67, paragraph 1, point b)
